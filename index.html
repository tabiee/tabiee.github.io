About Me

My name is Anna Rosputnia, although I typically go by Charlie. I am a game programmer from Ukraine, currently situated in Finland. I will soon be graduating from Jamk University of Applied Sciences with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Game Production.

Originally I started out as an artist and later on decided to focus my attention on game development. I've adored games as long as I can remember and eventually gained an interest in programming through mods and addons for various games, like Minecraft, World of Warcraft and Garry's Mod.

I love creating and problem-solving, and I am always curious to learn something new. Currently, I'm looking forward to getting my hands into the game industry and finding my place in the picture. So far, I have worked on a couple of games throughout my study years, primarily as a programmer and artist with occassional contributions to design, testing and whatever I could do to get progress going. Above are projects I have worked on, each of which thus far was made in Unity.
