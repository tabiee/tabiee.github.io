About Me

My name is Anna Rosputnia. I am a game programmer from Ukraine, currently situated in Finland. I will soon be graduating from Jamk University of Applied Sciences with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Game Production.

Originally I started out as an artist and later on decided to focus my attention on game development. I've adored games as long as I can remember and eventually gained an interest in programming through mods and addons for various games, like Minecraft, World of Warcraft and Garry's Mod.

I love creating and problem-solving, and I am always curious to learn something new. I'm looking forward to getting into interesting, creative projects and making fun experiences. So far, I have worked on a couple of games throughout my study years, primarily as a programmer and artist with occassional contributions to design, testing and whatever I could do to get progress going.
